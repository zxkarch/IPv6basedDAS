Vehicle Steering Detector
实时分析驾驶行为。

Research shows that most serious traffic accidents are caused by aggressive or abnormal steering behaviors. Detecting these behaviors is crucial for early warnings of serious traffic accidents. At present, vehicle steering behaviors can be detected by special systems, such as On-Board Diagnostic(OBD) and sensor based advanced driving assistance systems(ADAS), installed especially in high-end and luxury vehicles. As mobile electronic devices become increasingly powerful, our product aim at problem that vehicle steering detection.
